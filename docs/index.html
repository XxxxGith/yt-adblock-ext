FAQ

Why not just use a free ad blocker?

YouTube actively detects and blocks traditional ad blockers like uBlock Origin. YT Ad Cleaner uses a different approach that works reliably with the latest YouTube changes.

How many devices can I use?

Each license key can be activated on up to 3 devices. You can deactivate a device anytime to free up a slot.

What happens if I cancel?

Your license remains active until the end of the billing period. After that, the extension will stop blocking ads until you resubscribe.

Is my data safe?

We collect zero data. The extension runs entirely in your browser. License validation only sends your key to LemonSqueezy's API ‚Äî no browsing data, no analytics.

Does it work on other browsers?

Currently Chrome only. Firefox and Edge support coming soon.
